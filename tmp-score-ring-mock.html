ScoreRing — Approximate vs Final Score Indicators

How do we visually tell the user "this score is a rough estimate that will change after enrichment"?

Approach A: Ghost Range Arc

The solid arc shows the current partial score. A translucent "ghost" arc extends beyond it to show the potential range — "your score could land anywhere in this zone." After enrichment, the ghost disappears and the solid arc settles at the real score.

Approach B: Dashed Ring + Pulsing Glow

Partial scores use a dashed stroke pattern instead of solid — visually says "incomplete/estimated." A subtle pulse animation hints "more data coming." The tilde (~) prefix on the number reinforces it. After enrichment, the ring becomes solid green.

Approach C: Solid Half + Ghost Potential Zone

Combines the best of A and B. Solid yellow arc up to the partial score, then a faint dashed extension showing "your real score could reach up to here." The number uses a tilde. Clean, informative, not noisy.
